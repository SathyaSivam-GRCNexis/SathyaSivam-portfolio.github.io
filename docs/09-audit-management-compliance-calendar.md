# Audit Management & Compliance Calendar

**Project Type:** Audit Operations | **Platform:** SaaS GRC Console
**Scope:** Internal and external audit tracking, 5 calendar event sources

---

## What This Project Does

Audits are a fact of life for any organization that maintains security certifications. This project provides a centralized system for **scheduling, tracking, and completing** both internal and external audits -- paired with a unified compliance calendar that brings together every important deadline across the GRC platform into a single view.

The goal: no deadline is missed, no audit is unprepared for, and every stakeholder knows exactly what's due and when.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Before this system:

- Audit schedules were tracked in spreadsheets or personal calendars
- There was no central view of all compliance-related deadlines
- Different teams tracked their deadlines independently, creating silos
- Audit findings were logged in documents that weren't connected to the broader compliance system
- Internal audits lacked the same rigor and tracking as external audits
- It was difficult to coordinate evidence collection, gap remediation, and risk reviews around audit timelines

---

## Audit Types Tracked

### Internal Audits

Internal audits are conducted by the organization's own team to verify that security controls, processes, and procedures are working as intended.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Self-assessment to find and fix issues before external auditors do |
| **Frequency** | Typically quarterly or as defined by the compliance calendar |
| **Conducted By** | Internal audit team (with segregation of duties -- auditors don't audit their own work) |
| **Outputs** | Findings, corrective action plans, evidence review results |

### External Audits

External audits are conducted by independent third-party auditors (like BSI, TUV SUD, or Big 4 firms) to certify the organization against specific standards.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Purpose** | Independent verification for certification (ISO 27001, SOC 2, etc.) |
| **Frequency** | Annually or as required by the certification cycle |
| **Conducted By** | Independent accredited auditors |
| **Outputs** | Audit reports, certification decisions, non-conformities, observations |

---

## What the Audit Management System Tracks

For each audit, the system records:

- **Audit Type** -- Internal or External
- **Framework** -- Which standard is being audited (e.g., ISO 27001, SOC 2)
- **Schedule** -- Start date, end date, key milestones
- **Status** -- Planned, In Progress, Completed, Follow-Up Required
- **Auditor Information** -- Who is conducting the audit
- **Findings** -- Issues discovered during the audit
- **Corrective Actions** -- Plans to address each finding
- **Evidence Links** -- Connected to the evidence collection system for supporting documentation
- **Completion Statistics** -- Summary of results, pass/fail rates

## The Compliance Calendar

The compliance calendar is an **aggregate view** that brings together all due dates from across the GRC platform into one place:

### 5 Calendar Event Sources

| Source | What It Shows | Example |
|--------|---------------|---------|
| **Evidence Submission Deadlines** | When each piece of evidence is due | "Access control logs due March 15" |
| **Risk Review Dates** | When each risk must be reassessed | "Vendor risk review due April 1" |
| **Collection Cycle Periods** | Start and end of quarterly evidence collection | "Q2 2026 collection: April 1 -- June 30" |
| **Gap Assessment Schedules** | When gap assessments are planned | "ISO 27001 gap assessment: May 15" |
| **Audit Timelines** | When audits are scheduled | "SOC 2 Type II audit: June 10-20" |

### How the Calendar Helps

| Audience | What They See |
|----------|---------------|
| **GRC Analysts** | Their personal evidence deadlines and upcoming review tasks |
| **Team Leads** | Team-wide deadlines, audit preparation milestones, and resource planning |
| **Leadership** | High-level view of audit dates, certification renewal timelines |
| **Auditors** | Scheduled audit windows and pre-audit preparation status |

### Calendar Features

- **Single unified view** -- no more switching between multiple trackers
- **What's due** -- items approaching their deadline
- **What's overdue** -- items past their deadline (highlighted for attention)
- **What's coming next** -- upcoming deadlines for planning and preparation
- **Color-coded** by event type for quick visual scanning

## Audit Workflow

```
Planned --> Preparation --> In Progress --> Review Findings --> Close Out
                                                |
                                                v
                                        Corrective Actions
                                                |
                                                v
                                        Follow-Up Verification
```

| Phase | What Happens |
|-------|-------------|
| **Planned** | Audit is scheduled on the calendar with dates and scope |
| **Preparation** | Evidence is gathered, gaps are assessed, team is briefed |
| **In Progress** | Audit is being conducted (interviews, document review, testing) |
| **Review Findings** | Audit findings are logged and categorized |
| **Close Out** | Final report is issued, corrective actions are assigned |
| **Corrective Actions** | Fixes are implemented for each finding |
| **Follow-Up Verification** | Corrective actions are verified in the next audit cycle |
