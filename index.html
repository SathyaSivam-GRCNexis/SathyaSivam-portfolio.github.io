Hi, I'm Sathya — a compliance professional working across governance, risk, security, and privacy, helping organizations manage risk with clarity and practicality.

I began my career as an intern and grew into compliance through hands-on experience across audits, risk assessments, policy implementation, incident support, and cross-functional collaboration. My work goes beyond meeting requirements — it focuses on understanding how controls, processes, and people operate in real environments.

I've worked with frameworks including GDPR, HIPAA, SEBI, OWASP, and ISO-based management systems. While frameworks provide structure, my focus is on understanding the business risk behind each control and ensuring controls are applied consistently and meaningfully. As a woman in tech, I'm committed to continuous learning and raising standards within the GRC industry.
